# GreenMIR Dataset

Source: "Green MIR? Investigating computational cost of recent music-AI research in ISMIR" (Holzapfel et al., ISMIR 2024)

## Corpus 1 — Automatic Music Generation (corpus1.xlsx)

- **80 articles** from ISMIR (2017-2023)
- Focus: automatic music generation papers
- Only **21/80** articles report computational resources
- Columns: Year, Title, Link, Countries, Resources (free text), Number of parameters, TDP/W, Number of GPUs, Training time (hours), Energy cost (kWh)

## Corpus 2 — Computing-heavy Music Analysis (corpus2.xlsx)

- **33 articles** from ISMIR (2022-2023)
- Focus: papers that explicitly use GPUs
- More complete than corpus 1 (selected because they mention GPU usage)
- Columns: Year, Title, Link, GPU* (free text), TDP/W, Number of GPUs, Training time (hours), Energy cost (kWh)

## Total: 113 articles (IDs 1-80 for corpus 1, 81-113 for corpus 2)
