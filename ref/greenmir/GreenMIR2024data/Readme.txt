This archive contains the data used in the paper "Green MIR? Investigating computational cost of recent music-AI research in ISMIR", published in the ISMIR 2024 proceedings. If you use this data, please cite our paper as follows:

@InProceedings{Holzapfel2024ISMIR,
  author    = {Andre Holzapfel and Anna-Kaisa Kaila and Petra Jääskeläinen},
  title     = {Green MIR? Investigating computational cost of recent music-AI research in ISMIR},
  booktitle = {International Society for Music Information Retrieval Conference, ISMIR},
  year      = {2024},
}

The data is divided into two spreadhsheets, each containing the data of one of the two datasets used in our paper. Spreadsheets are provided as ms-excel and pdf. The speadsheets contain an explanation of column content in the second row.
